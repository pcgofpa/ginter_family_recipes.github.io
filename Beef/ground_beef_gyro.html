Easy Ground Beef Gyros with Tzatziki Sauce

Ingredients

Gyro Meat

  • 2 lbs. ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons
    1. dried oregano
    2. ground thyme
    3. ground cumin
    4. garlic powder
    5. onion powder
    6. salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 8 pitas
  • Optional Toppings:
    1. lettuce
    2. sliced tomato
    3. sliced cucumber
    4. sliced red onion
    5. fresh dill
    6. feta cheese

Tzatziki Sauce

  • 1.5 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1.5 cup grated cucumber
  • 4 tablespoons fresh dill, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 cup feta cheese, crumbled

Steps

  1. Add the ground beef to a skillet over medium-high heat and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Add the seasonings and ½ cup of water. Simmer over medium heat until the sauce has thickened around the meat, about 5 minutes.
  2. While the beef cooks, make the Tzatziki sauce. Start by squeezing the excessive liquid out of the grated cucumber, discarding the liquid. In a small bowl, mix the grated cucumber with the remaining ingredients.
  3. To assemble a gyro, layer a pita with lettuce, tomato, cucumber, onion, and ground beef, then finish with tzatziki sauce, fresh dill and feta cheese.
